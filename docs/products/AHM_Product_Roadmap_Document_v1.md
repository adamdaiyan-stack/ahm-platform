AHM PLATFORM — PRODUCT ROADMAP DOCUMENT
Version 1.0
Strategic Product Development Roadmap

1. PLATFORM OBJECTIVE

The AHM Platform is being built as:
“Pakistan’s Financial Intelligence Operating System.”

The platform will combine:
- brokerage infrastructure
- investment intelligence
- AI-powered research
- client onboarding
- portfolio management
- institutional analytics
- retail usability

The objective is NOT to create:
- a basic brokerage website
- a collection of dashboards
- a simple market viewer

The objective IS to create:
- a scalable financial ecosystem
- a data-rich intelligence platform
- a future AI-powered financial operating system

The platform must evolve in layers.

1. Foundation
2. Operations
3. Intelligence

- uncontrolled expansion
- frontend-first architecture
- technical debt accumulation
